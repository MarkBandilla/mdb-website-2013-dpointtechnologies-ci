Mark Bandilla

Designer at heart. Mark walked into the tubes of the internet while in high school. Designing posters, business cards, fliers and websites for local business.

While taking up Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science at Informatics International College he served as the Layout Designer for mural painting at the Rizal Technological University and won 1st place in C Language Programming Contest. He is enthusiastic and a pro-active Web Developer and Solution Architect who makes easy to use website without compromising the functionality. He has a huge interest in application design in an agile way by quickly listening to feedback of end users and clients. With love for clean usable interfaces in the front end and powerful and interactive back end interfaces in web applications. He makes the web a better place. With more that 9 years experience of designing and developing websites and web applications, where as he have an excellent understanding of how technology can be used to align the requirements of both the business and the customers.
